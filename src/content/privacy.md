# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is Transpo's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website and extension.

## 1. Information We Collect
We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. For example, when you sign up for early access, we collect your email address.

## 2. Audio Processing
Our browser extension performs audio analysis locally on your machine. **No audio data is ever transmitted, recorded, or stored on our servers.**

## 3. How We Use Information
We use your email address solely to notify you about product launches, important updates, and account-related matters. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

## 4. Security
We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.
